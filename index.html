Metal recycling & forging machines

Metal shredders, hydraulic steel forging presses, metal recycling industrial equipment. Metal shredders are machines used for processing a wide range of metal scrap. About

Changzhou Kangyun Machinery Technology Co., LTD established in 2021 at the city of Changzhou by me -Glukhov Pavel, Russian citizen.

Actually, first business between Chinese and Russian companies began at 2018 year.

At the beginning it was just small orders for food processing equipment from friends and family, (as my sister has a company in Moscow) made through taobao app. or alibaba app. (ex. Grinders, electric sieves, oil making machines, drying chambers, vacuum machines, packing equipment). Raw materials are: fruits, vegetables, berries, nuts, seeds, mushrooms Later after “PRODEXPO Moscow 2020” business meeting we started to do business with Bakeries, Biotechenergy company, Biotika-Karellia company, Zagotprom company () - the Russian enterprise specializing in harvesting, cleaning and processing of forest berries collected on the territory of the Republic of Karelia.

We have good relationship with reliable cargo company since that times, that has warehouses at Beijing, Wuxi, Guangzhou (Cargo9533). Another logistics partner is Russian “ACD logistics” company.

All the customers are very glad to cooperate and use that equipment, so more people started to ask me for assistance in doing business with China, language barrier is still a communication trouble for many companies.

Projects

Oilfield

In 2020 Russian company “Innovative Industrial Company LLC.” signed an agreement with me to find reliable supplier in China for oilfield equipment. After negotiating with more than 10 factories, we finally signed a contract with “Jiangsu Hongxunoil equipment Co. LTD” (www.hongxunoil.com).
